<objective>
Wire both hardening features into the rod-cli runtime, gated on the Plan-01 toggles and driven by a stable session seed:
- **HARDEN-01 WebRTC:** call `opts.WithWebRTCLeakProtection(true)` in `launchBrowser` (browser-pref leg) and `em.EvadeWebRTC()` in `createPage` (JS leg), both gated on `cfg.Stealth.WebRTCLeakProtection`.

Purpose: This is the integration seam that makes Plan 01 (config) and Plan 02 (godoll engine) actually take effect on the live browser. The harness (Plan 04) then asserts no leaked host IP and stable canvas hashes.
Output: `noiseSeed` field + once-only generation on Context; gated `WithWebRTCLeakProtection` in launchBrowser; gated `EvadeWebRTC` + `SetNoiseSeed` + SpoofCanvas/SpoofAudioContext in createPage; `profileFromStealth` canvas/audio gating.
</objective>
